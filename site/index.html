how it works

Six things. Nothing more.

Every feature earns its place. If it adds friction, it doesn't ship.

01

Instant capture

Sub-300ms from tap to cursor. The keyboard is already up. Type, speak, or sketch — then close. That's the whole interaction.

02

The noodle feed

A reverse-chronological stream of raw thoughts. Each entry is text and a timestamp. No tags, no folders, no decisions required.

03

Untangle mode

AI surfaces connections between entries — patterns you haven't consciously made. A thinking partner you choose to engage, not a background process.

04

Gentle nudges

Smart prompts reconnect old thoughts with recent ones before they go stale. Not reminders — genuine, interesting connections.

05

Voice & sketch

Whatever's fastest in the moment. Up to 10 minutes of voice on Pro. Rough sketches and diagrams for visual thinkers.

06

Shared boards

Share a noodle board with anyone via link. They see your thinking without logging in. To add their own thought — they need the app.
