Frequently asked questions

When is Noodle launching?

We're currently in closed beta. Join the waitlist to get early access — we're rolling out invites in waves.

Which platforms are supported?

Noodle is iOS-first. We're launching on iPhone and iPad, with Apple Watch on Pro. Android and web are on the roadmap.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your thoughts are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data. AI features are opt-in and only process content when you ask. Read our privacy policy and AI policy for details.

Can I export my data?

Pro users can export to Notion, Obsidian, and Apple Notes. All users can request a full data export by contacting us.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device. You'll keep Pro features until the end of your billing period.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Use the contact form below with "Bug report" as the subject. Include as much detail as you can — device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce help us fix things faster.
